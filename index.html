What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The food and service has to be even better than the old canteen, so we have decided to upgrade our food to healthier and tastier food for our students. We also have to keep the canteen clean, so we decided to put garbage cans next to each table.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Manage System

    Attendance is very important so we decided to make the attendance system better. We have made the system so if you join the class the teacher has to log that information to a website called Infinite Campus, where the attendance will be sent to the parents.
